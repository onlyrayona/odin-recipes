Pumpkin Soup Recipe

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 can pumpkin
  • 2 cans chicken stock
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 medium onion
  • 2 tablespoons butter

Steps

  1. Mince onion and cook in butter in saucepan until soft
  2. Add chicken broth. Heat to boil then turn down heat to simmer
  3. Add pumpkin and simmer for 40 minutes
  4. Stir in heavy cream
  5. Add salt and pepper to taste
